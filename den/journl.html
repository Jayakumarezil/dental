Myself Dr. Preethi Rajamanickam, PG resident, Saveetha Dental College and Hospitals

I’m based in CHENNAI, had always loved working with small mechanical models and kits and had a natural artistic propensity during my bachelorette degree. I was also aware that, of my peers, orthodontists seemed to enjoy a happy work-life balance. Apart from all these, I feel that it is a blessing to recreate someone’s smile. These factors cemented a plan to pursue my post-doctorate studies in orthodontics.

The first step was graduation from Tagore Dental College and Hospitals with a Bachelor of Dental Surgery degree.

After graduating with a bachelor degree in dentistry, I spent few years in general clinical practice under the guidance of renowned dentists. Over time, I became more fascinated with dentistry and wanted to pursue deeper knowledge and learning with a master degree in Saveetha Dental College and Hospitals
